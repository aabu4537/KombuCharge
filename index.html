About KombuCharge

KombuCharge is dedicated to crafting high-quality kombucha drinks that are not only delicious but also beneficial for your well-being. Our team of experts is passionate about creating unique flavors using natural and organic ingredients. We believe in the power of kombucha to invigorate your body and mind. This kombucha is brewed with Yerba Mate tea, providing 100mg of caffeine for an energizing boost. We use organic cane sugar or honey as an additional substitute to lower the grams of sugar utilized.

Our commitment to quality is reflected in our certifications. KombuCharge is USDA certified Organic, Certified Alcohol Extracted, Non GMO certified, and Certified Gluten Free.
